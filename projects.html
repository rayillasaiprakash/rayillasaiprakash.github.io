Ongoing Research & Digital Humanities Initiative

Telangana Language Documentation

An integrated documentation programme focused on Telangana's regional language varieties, oral traditions, community knowledge, cultural practices, and intangible heritage.

Connected Subprojects

  • Telangana Voices: Digital Language & Culture Archive
    A digital archive documenting regional speech varieties, oral narratives, local vocabulary, community knowledge, and cultural expression across Telangana.
  • Telangana Proverbs Archive
    An ongoing fieldwork-based corpus documenting proverbs with original Telugu forms, translations, speaker interpretations, contexts of use, cultural meanings, semantic themes, figurative devices, and linguistic observations.
  • Telangana Festivals Documentation
    Community-based documentation of local festivals, rituals, oral accounts, regional terminology, agricultural traditions, and associated cultural practices in Telangana.
