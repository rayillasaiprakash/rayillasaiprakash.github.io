Telangana Voices

A Digital Archive of Telangana Language, Culture, and Folk Knowledge

Telangana Voices is an expanding independent documentation project devoted to Telangana Telugu vocabulary, dialect variation, oral traditions, proverbs, festivals, and everyday cultural knowledge.

Explore the Archive

Telangana Proverbs Archive

A growing corpus of Telangana Telugu proverbs collected through fieldwork, with speaker explanations, translations, cultural contexts, and linguistic analysis.

TT–ST Lexicon

A developing lexical resource comparing Telangana Telugu (TT) forms with Standard Telugu (ST) equivalents and English glosses.

Telangana Festivals Documentation

Documentation of festivals, ritual vocabulary, cultural practices, and oral traditions.

Future Collections

Planned documentation of dialect expressions, speaker narratives, folk knowledge, and region-specific cultural vocabulary.
