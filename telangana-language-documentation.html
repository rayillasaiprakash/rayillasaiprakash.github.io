ONGOING RESEARCH & DIGITAL HUMANITIES INITIATIVE

Telangana Language Documentation

Documenting Telangana's regional language varieties, oral traditions, community knowledge, and cultural heritage through fieldwork, corpus development, digital archiving, translation, and cultural interpretation.

Telangana Voices

Digital Language & Culture Archive

A digital archive for regional speech varieties, oral narratives, local vocabulary, community knowledge, and cultural expression. The initiative aims to preserve community voices while making documentation accessible for linguistic and cultural research.

Telangana Proverbs Archive

An ongoing fieldwork-based corpus of Telangana Telugu proverbs. Entries document the original proverb, translation, speaker explanation, idiomatic meaning, context of use, themes, semantic domains, figurative devices, cultural interpretation, and linguistic observations.

Telangana Festivals Documentation

Community-based documentation of local festivals and life-cycle practices, including oral accounts, ritual terminology, agricultural traditions, regional vocabulary, cultural meanings, and locally transmitted knowledge.

Documentation Approach

  • Community-based fieldwork and speaker accounts
  • Audio, textual, lexical, and ethnographic documentation
  • Transcription, translation, annotation, and contextual interpretation
  • Searchable corpus and digital-archive development
  • Ethical representation of community knowledge and speaker perspectives

Research Significance

The programme connects dialectology, sociolinguistics, language documentation, folklore, digital humanities, and cultural heritage research. It extends earlier work on regional variation in the Karimnagar dialect into a broader, ongoing documentation agenda centred on Telangana communities.
