All India Conference of Dravidian Linguists (AICDL) 2024

Presentation Title

Role of Perception in Spelling Errors: A Case of Telugu Primary School Children


This presentation examined the influence of perception on spelling errors among Telugu-speaking primary school children. It explored linguistic and educational perspectives on spelling development and highlighted the relationship between spoken language and written forms.
